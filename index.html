Direct Admission in SRM University

Now get direct admission in B.Tech / BE / M.Tech in SRM University, Chennai through institute level vacant seats and management quota seats. The minimum eligibility criteria required for the admission in SRM University is 45% marks in Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics at 10+2 Board Examination.

Direct Admission in SRMUniversity Through Management Quota Eligibility Criteria:

  • For Engineering you are must above 16 years old.
  • You must have scored 50% of marks in 12th boards examination.
  • If you want to apply for B.tech in Genetic Engineering, Biomedical Engineering and Biotechnology Engineering program you must have to secure 50% total mark either in PCM or PCB (Physics, Chemistry, Biology).
  • Eligibility criteria for Direct Admission in SRM University through Management Quota is based on the counseling on your rank scored in the SRMJEEE entrance exams.
  • Depending on your rank score and 12th board examination marks you will get Admission in SRM Colleges.
